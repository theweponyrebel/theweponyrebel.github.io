Pier fishing in Hong Kong

Most people who fish in Hong Kong fish from a pier. If you want to catch decent to big sized fish from the pier the most recommended place by locals is Sai Kung or Lamma Island. Stanley pier is a gret place to catch palm sized fish and every once in a while someone catch a big fish. I go to Tai tam usually at Tai Tam you can catch big fish I have seen people catch big fish there. The first time I was there I caught a 6 inch lizard fish and I saw someone catch a tiny grouper. Lots of people don't even use a rod while fishing from the pier they jusr use a line with squid on a hook and when the fishing come toward the squid they stap the fish with the hook and bring it to the surface.

Freshwater Fishing

Hong Kong offers a good freshwater fishing in 17 reservoirs. The fish here includes Koi, Gold fish, Largemouth bass, Carp and Catfish. Their is a liscene you need for fishing here and its 30 HKD and you can only fish there between September 1 and March 31 during non spawning season. The Fishing here is better than in the Saltwater pier fishing because the fish are often bigger and there is no pollution in the reservoirs. The equitment you need for the reservoirs you need is the same fo saltwater just different bait.

Deep Sea Fish and Oil Rig Fishing

There are lots of charter that will take you deep sea fishing in Hong Kong. Deep fishing is the thing for you if you want to catch the big one. The fish caught while deep sea fishing here is Mackrel, Tuna, Mahi Mahi, Grouper, Barracuda, Marlin, Sailfish, Snapper and even a grey Shark. Deep sea fishing charter take you out in the waters surrounding Hong Kong for the whole day. The Technique while deep se fishing is trolling. If you are super serious you can take a charter out to the South China oil rigs which is a 9 hour boat trip from Hong Kong. Here you will usually spend a couple of days fishing and you can catch about the same fish if you were to go deep sea fishing in Hong Kong just more fish and bigger fish!.
