Fly Fishing

Fly Fishing is a type of Fishing technique that is very popular fishing technique in Colorado. Fly fishing is not always done at the surface sometimes you can fly fish as deep as 20 ft. In Colorado you mainly fly fish for trout in rivers. When fly fishing you can use different types of lures some lures sink and some float. You cabn put a special powder a lure to float.
